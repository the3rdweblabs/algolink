# Impact Projects

## AID Tech

AID:Tech is a blockchain-based social impact enterprise focused on transforming how humanitarian aid, disaster relief, and programmable payments are delivered—using transparent, efficient, and fraud-resistant systems. It builds infrastructure for stablecoin disbursements, digital identity verification, and programmable payouts (e.g., funds with rules like expiration dates, spending categories, or conditions). Their key product is the Kare Wallet (or Kare Survivor Wallet), a smart wallet that enables instant aid distribution to disaster survivors or beneficiaries, often in USDC stablecoins or other digital assets.

- AID Tech Website: https://www.aid.technology/
- AID Tech X account: https://x.com/aidtechnology

## Hesab Pay

HesabPay is a digital payments platform (mobile wallet and e-money service) primarily operating in Afghanistan, with expansion into Syria and other regions. It serves as the country's first and only interoperable digital payments solution, enabling users to send/receive money, pay bills (like electricity), buy airtime, handle remittances, and more — all accessible via smartphone apps (iOS/Android), web browser, USSD for feature phones, or QR code cards.

- HesabPay Website: https://hesab.com/
- HesabPay X: https://x.com/HesabPay_

## Whole Chain

Wholechain is a blockchain-based supply chain traceability platform that uses Algorand as its underlying blockchain to provide end-to-end visibility, trust, and transparency in fragmented supply chains. Wholechain enables businesses (from global brands and manufacturers to small cooperatives) to track products at the item level—from origin/raw materials through manufacturing, distribution, and retail—while recording verifiable data immutably on the Algorand blockchain. This creates tamper-proof, transparent records that help verify claims about product origins, sustainability, ethical sourcing, compliance (e.g., food safety standards, anti-child labor norms), and authenticity.

- Wholechain website: https://wholechain.com/
