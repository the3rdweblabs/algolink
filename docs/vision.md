# The AlgoLink Vision: identity first

At its core, **AlgoLink is an identity layer for the Algorand blockchain**. While we provide the tools to explore the ledger, our primary mission is to solve the "Human Gap" in Web3 - making addresses recognizable and interactions personal.

## 🛡️ Identity over Expiration
The mission of AlgoLink is to provide a **universal, persistent, and accessible identity layer**. We believe that your digital presence shouldn't be a temporary lease.

### The Problem we Solve
Traditional blockchain naming services (such as ANS) often operate on a "rental" model. Users must pay significant fees to "own" a name for a limited time. If they stop paying, their identity - and the links established across the ecosystem - disappear.

### The AlgoLink Solution
AlgoLink is designed to be **Identity-as-a-Service, not Domain-as-a-Lease**.
-   **No Expiration**: Once you link your email to your wallet, that link is persistent. You don't "lose" your identity because you forgot a renewal date.
-   **Zero to Low Cost**: We focus on high accessibility. Our goal is to ensure even the smallest wallet holder can have a recognized identity on-chain.

---

## 🤝 Relationship with Algo Name Service (ANS)

It is important to clarify that **AlgoLink is not a replacement for ANS**.

Algo Name Service serves a specific, valuable purpose in the ecosystem as a premier naming protocol. We view our platform as a **complementary solution** that addresses different user needs:

| Feature | Algo Name Service (ANS) | AlgoLink |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Model** | Domain NFT Lease | Identity Linking |
| **Cost** | Recurring Lease Fees | Persistent / Free |
| **Duration** | Expires (Time-bound) | Permanent |
| **Focus** | Short, readable names (.algo) | Verified Cross-app identity |
| **Discovery** | Metadata Search | Smart Aggregated Search |

We believe there is room for both. Users who want the prestige and short-form readability of a `.algo` domain should use ANS. Users who want a secure, no-hassle, permanent link between their email and their collection—without the "rental" anxiety—should use AlgoLink.

---

## 🚀 The Future
Our vision is for AlgoLink to disappear into the background. We want dApps, explorers, and marketplaces to simply "know" who a user is by resolving their AlgoLink identity via their email or wallet, creating a more social and human experience across the entire Algorand network.
